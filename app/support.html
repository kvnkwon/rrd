Changing your account email address

To update the email address associated with your ResumeRabbit account:

  1. Go to Account > Email Settings
  2. Click on the field displaying your current email address
  3. Enter your new email address
  4. Click save to confirm changes

Changing your account password

Follow these instructions to change your password while logged in:

  1. Go to Account > Change password
  2. Enter your old password, new password, and confirm your new password
  3. Click submit to confirm changes
